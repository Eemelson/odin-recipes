Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the most authentic Italian food

Everyone loves lasagna, here's how to do it

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Cut and saute onions, carrots and celery
  2. Fry minced beef
  3. Add minced garlic at the end, fry the garlic for a few minutes, then add tomato sauce
  4. Bring up to simmer and let it sit for 30 minutes
  5. Assemble lasagna
