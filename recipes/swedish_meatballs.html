Swedish Meatballs

Description

A certified classic straight from Sweden

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 dl breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 4 dl milk
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Add minced beef, diced onion, minced garlic, breadcrumbs and egg to a bowl and mix thoroughly
  2. Make approx. 2 cm diameter sized balls from the mixture
  3. Fry the balls in a pan with oil until golden brown all around
  4. Make roux
